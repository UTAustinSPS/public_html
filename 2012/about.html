Who are we?

We are the University of Texas at Austin chapter of the Society of Physics Students or SPS. We are a student run organization that brings together physics and non-physics (but still interested in physics) students here at UT Austin to promote collaboration and circulate information between fellow. Our regular meetings typically feature a faculty member and his or her respective research, but it could be about anything. For those of you not convinced, all of our meetings have free food.

SPS also produces the wonderful physics shirts you will see around campus. Each semester we take new ideas so if you ever wanted to be the designer of an iconic piece of UT Physics apparel you should definitely poke around a meeting when it is shirt sales time.

If in case you are not able to come to our meetings but still want to meet and talk to physics majors at UT we definitely encourage you to visit the Physics Undergraduate Lounge in RLM 5.222. SPS helps to maintain this room and rents the lockers out to those who want the convenient storage.

This semester we will be meeting Monday Nights in RLM 5.120 at 6:00pm
