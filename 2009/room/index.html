Physics Undergraduate Lounge

RLM 5.222

The Physics Undergraduate Lounge is located next to the Kodosky Reading Room and opposite to Pat Morgan's office on the 5th floor. It is open to all undergraduate physics majors. See Pat Morgan or one of the officers about getting access, or come to a meeting !

The room features:

  • Coffee! 
  • Lockers: Bring your own lock. Label your locker with your name and the current school year.
  • Refrigerator, Microwave and Sink: Bring your lunch and keep it cold in SPS refrigerator. Lunch hot or cold. Open sodas and leftover pizza are free for the taking. The sink is new; kindly do not dispose of food in it, or it will clog.
  • Food and Drink: Available for purchase on the honor system. Check the fridge and tables for reasonably priced sodas, snacks, and other things. 
  • Library: There are many books ranging from incredibly useful (entry level references) to generally unhelpful (bad fiction or graduate level). We ask that you keep items in the room. 
  • Computer: There is a working old school Apple G2 with internet access.
  • Phone: In the era before the cell phone, this was perhaps a really big deal, but keeping with the old guide, "Don't pay for (local) phone calls! To make an on-campus call from the lounge, dial the last five digits of the number. To make an off-campus call from the lounge, dial 9 first, then the local number. Campus Directory Information: 471-3434."
Note: there is free coffee available in the Kodosky Reading Room, but you are required to drink it there, and partaking of food is not allowed.  Also, for you tea drinkers and french press users, there is boiling hot water out of the coffee brewer.
