Spring Physnic 2003 - Friday, May 2 - 4:30PM

The Spring Physnic will be held at Eastwoods Park, located a couple of blocks from RLM. There will be barbecue to eat and plenty of activites for you to enjoy. The cost is $5 for students and $8 for faculty. Tickets can be purchased from Pat Morgan in RLM 5.216 or any of the SPS officers. Tickets will also be sold at the Physnic.

SPS Membership Information

Here's the thing, you wanna be a member? You're a member. One of these days we're going to charge dues, but not this semester. So enjoy it while it lasts. I should also mention that we do not require that you be a member at the national level, though it is preferred, and we think you'll like the benefit of receiving Physics Today. To apply for the national organization, please pick up a form from on top of the filing cabinet in the SPS lounge (RLM 5.222) and send it to the listed address, along with $15.00.
