The objectives of the Society of Physics Students shall be to:

  • a) encourage and assist student physicists to develop the knowledge, competence,
            enthusiasm, and social responsibility that are essential to the advancement of physics;

  • b) stimulate interest in advanced study and research in physics;

  • c) develop friendship among physics students and faculty members;

  • d) popularize interest in physics in the general collegiate public;

  • e) recognize the attainment of high scholarship and potential acheivement
            in physics among outstanding students by means of Sigma Pi Sigma membership.

    *Excerpted from Article III of the Constitution of The Society of Physics Students, 1968.
