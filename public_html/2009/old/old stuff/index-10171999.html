SPS Membership Information

During the spring, local dues were not required. This may change in the future, but for now this means that all it takes to be a member is to let one of the officers know that you're interested, and then you're instantly in. We also do not require that you be a member at the national level, though it is preferred, and we think you'll like the benefit of receiving Physics Today. To apply for the national organization, please pick up a form from on top of the filing cabinet in the SPS lounge (RLM 5.222) and send it to the listed address, along with $15.00.

You might also want to sign up for our mailing list. Send email to listproc@mcfeeley.cc.utexas.edu with no subject and

"subscribe sps email@mail.utexas.edu FirstName LastName"

as the first and only body line.
