SPS Membership Information

Here's the thing, you wanna be a member? You're a member. One of these days we're going to charge dues, but not this semester. So enjoy it while it lasts. I should also mention that we do not require that you be a member at the national level, though it is preferred, and we think you'll like the benefit of receiving Physics Today. To apply for the national organization, please pick up a form from on top of the filing cabinet in the SPS lounge (RLM 5.222) and send it to the listed address, along with $15.00. Or, if you are an internet junkie like us... Sign up online! at
