Physics Open House

Have a Poster? Sign up to present!

The Physics Open House is held every fall in November. It will be held November 14, 2016 for Fall 2016! It is a chance for us undergraduates to show off the research we have been working on!There is also an undegraduate Poster Competition with cash prizes! To register for the Open House Poster Contest, contact one of the SPS officers.
