T Shirt Design Instructions

  1. Create design (Spec Sheet)
    Must be original, single sided, centered, and require few colors
  2. Submit design
    Fill out disclaimer acknowledging rules by October 2nd, 23:39
    Submit artwork by email: SPSTshirts@gmail.com (Also by October 2nd, 23:59 although continue to submit them during the year as we will still be accepting them)
    No maximum number of (wholly unique) submissions
    Must be 18 to submit
    Ordered filetype preference:
    Adobe Illustrator (.AI)
    Adobe Photoshop (.PSD)
    High Resolution PNGs (150-300dpi)
    High Resolution JPEGs (150-300dpi)
    Note: Designs using more colors will cost more
    Note: Designs including the UT logo will also cost more
  3. Voting
    SPS will vote on the winning designs at the meeting of October 3rd.
    (Discounted) preorders will commence
    The target price on these will be in the neighborhood of $15.
    (Specifics depend on the design, the quantity preordered, and the quote from the printer.)
    Each winning design yields a free Tshirt for its submitter!
  4. Final steps
    T shirts are printed and delivered
    Full-price shirts sales commence
