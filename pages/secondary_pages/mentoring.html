So you want the ins and outs?

Join a mentoring group and find what's happening

Mentoring is taking a new spin this semester. There are going to be streams that encompass our successful workshops and add some new ones. We are going to have a Journal Club to introduce each other to different physics topics, the Machine Shop Workshop to build skills in making experimental apparatus, and an Electronics Workshop to build skills in soldiering and circuit design. All in all this is a great way for you learn about several new and helpful things to progress yourself and your physics career.

Mentoring can count towards Sigma Pi Sigma service hours. Talk to the Sigma Pi Sigma Representative for more details.
