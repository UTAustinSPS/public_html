So you want to join Honors Groups?

You should join Sigma Pi Sigma and be part of the Greatness.


Sigma Pi Sigma is one of the few Honors organizations here on campus that service Physics majors. Despite the implcation that it is exculsively for Physics Majors, Sigma Pi Sigma is for people in any major. You just have to take some Physics classes.

Here are the details for your convenience:

All members-elect shall have completed 60 credit hours towards an undergraduate degree.

All members-elect shall have completed 15 credit hours of physics courses.

All members-elect shall have attained a 6.5 combined grade point average (GPA), taking the sum of the cumulative GPA on a 4.0 scale and the physics-only GPA on a 4.0 scale, with a minimum of a 3.0 physics-only GPA. Rounding should be done to the tenths place to calculate the combined GPA (e.g., a 6.495 combined score or above will qualify for this requirement).

All members-elect shall have completed 10 hours of SPS- or Sigma Pi Sigma-organized or approved service during both the Fall and Spring semester prior to induction and all following semesters (for a total of 20 hours per year), including, but not limited to:

  • Teaching students in elementary, middle, or high schools
  • Volunteering for events like the Physics Open House, Explore UT, or Prospective Students Day
  • Tutoring fellow classmates during Sunday Night Study Sessions
  • Acting as a mentor for SPS Mentoring groups
  • Aiding in projects or activities sponsored by SPS or Sigma Pi Sigma.

All members-elect shall be registered members of the National Society of Physics Students.

All members-elect shall have obtained the approval to join Sigma Pi Sigma from the Sigma Pi Sigma Chapter Advisor based on the requirements listed in this document. The Sigma Pi Sigma Chapter Advisor may allow for special circumstances with regard to requirements.

All members are expected to uphold the requirements above once inducted. Failure to comply with requirements above will result in termination of membership in Sigma Pi Sigma, with the following mutually-exclusive exceptions:

  • Combined GPA falls below 6.5, but not below 6.0 in a semester other than the semester in which the member is graduating. The member will become a provisional member for a maximum of one semester until all requirements are again fulfilled.
  • Community service time as outlined in requirement 4 falls below 10 hours/semester, but not below 5 hours/semester in a semester other than the semester in which the member is graduating. The member will become a provisional member for a maximum of one semester until all requirements are again fulfilled.
  • Special circumstances as approved by the Sigma Pi Sigma Chapter Advisor
